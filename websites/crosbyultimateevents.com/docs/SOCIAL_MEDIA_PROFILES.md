## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Professional yet warm** - Expert but approachable
- **Luxury without pretension** - Premium quality, accessible communication
- **Confident and reassuring** - "We've got this, you can relax"
- **Detail-oriented** - Emphasize meticulous planning and attention to detail

### Language Style
- Use "we" and "you" to create connection
- Emphasize outcomes: "flawless events," "stress-free," "memorable experiences"
- Avoid jargon; use clear, benefit-focused language
- Include Southern hospitality touches (for Houston market)

### Content Themes
1. **Behind-the-scenes** - Event setup, menu preparation, chef at work
2. **Client success stories** - Event highlights, testimonials (with permission)
3. **Planning tips** - Event planning advice, menu ideas, vendor coordination tips
4. **Culinary artistry** - Food photography, menu showcases, chef expertise
5. **Stress-free hosting** - Benefits of professional planning, time-saving tips
