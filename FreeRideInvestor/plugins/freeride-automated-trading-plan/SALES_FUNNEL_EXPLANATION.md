# FreeRideInvestor Sales Funnel - How It Works

## The Plugin IS Your Sales Funnel

This plugin is a **complete sales funnel system** that:

### 1. **Generates the Product** (Daily Trading Plans)
- Automatically creates trading plans every day
- Uses your TradingView strategy (MA, RSI, risk management)
- Generates TBOW tactic posts for SEO/content

### 2. **Restricts Access** (Free vs Premium)
- Free users: Can't see plans (see upgrade prompts)
- Premium users: Full access to all plans
- Creates urgency and value proposition

### 3. **Provides Sales Pages**
- **Premium Signup Page** (`/premium-signup`) - The sales page
- **Trading Plans Page** (`/trading-plans`) - The product showcase
- **Access Denied Pages** - Conversion points

### 4. **Automates Everything**
- Daily plan generation (cron job)
- TBOW post creation
- User role management
- Access control

## Sales Funnel Flow

```
Visitor
  ↓
Sees Trading Plan Preview / "Access Denied"
  ↓
Sign Up (Free Account)
  ↓
Sees Upgrade Prompt
  ↓
Premium Signup Page
  ↓
Payment / Upgrade
  ↓
Premium Access → View All Plans
```

## What Makes It a Sales Funnel

1. **Lead Generation**: Free signup captures emails
2. **Value Demonstration**: Shows what they're missing (access denied)
3. **Conversion Point**: Premium signup page with pricing
4. **Product Delivery**: Daily plans for premium members
5. **Retention**: Fresh content every day keeps them subscribed

## Integration with Your Site

The plugin integrates with:

- Your existing content (TBOW posts appear in blog)
