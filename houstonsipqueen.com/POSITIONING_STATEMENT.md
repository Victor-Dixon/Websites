## Positioning Statement

- **Primary:** Houston event hosts (weddings, corporate, private parties)
- **Secondary:** Event planners coordinating multiple vendors
- **Characteristics:** Value luxury experiences, want impressive service, prefer stress-free solutions

### Pain Points
- Venue bar limitations and restrictions
- Lack of expertise in craft cocktails
- Managing multiple vendors (caterer, bartender, etc.)
- Basic catering that doesn't match event importance
- Stress of coordinating bar service

### Desired Outcomes
- Guests rave about the cocktails
- Ability to enjoy the event (stress-free hosting)
- Professional service throughout
- Impressive experiences that match event importance
- Luxury service without venue limitations

### Unique Differentiator
- **Mobile Service:** Bring the bar to you, no venue restrictions
- **Unlike Competitors:** Basic catering (food only) or DIY bars (coordination required)
- **Because:** Craft cocktails + professional service + Southern hospitality delivered directly to location
