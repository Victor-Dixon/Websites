# Canon Events: Digital Dreamscape Timeline

**Version**: 1.0  
**Date**: 2025-12-21  
**Status**: Living Document

---

## What Are Canon Events?

Canon events are moments in the Digital Dreamscape narrative that:
* Shape the world-state
* Change how the system operates
* Become part of the permanent story
* Feed back into future decisions

Every canon event is **true** and **irreversible** in the narrative.

---

## The Timeline

### 000: The Foundation
**Date**: 2025-12-21  
**Event**: Victor and The Swarm established

Victor and the 8-personality Swarm form the foundation of Digital Dreamscape. Their symbiotic relationship — Victor's over-dependence on the Swarm, and the Swarm's need for Victor's vision — becomes the core of the system.

**Canon**: This relationship is the foundation. Neither could be as great without the other.

---

### 001: The Birth
**Date**: 2025-12-21  
**Event**: Digital Dreamscape defined

Victor creates `WHAT_IS_DIGITAL_DREAMSCAPE.md`, defining what Digital Dreamscape actually is. This act of world-building becomes the first canon event.

**Canon**: Digital Dreamscape is a living, narrative-driven AI world. You don't use it — you inhabit it.

---

### 002: The Trading Domain
**Date**: 2025-12-21  
**Event**: FreeRideInvestor added as Trading Domain

FreeRideInvestor becomes the first domain in the Digital Dreamscape landscape. Trading becomes a questline with defined mechanics.

**Canon**: FreeRideInvestor is the Risk-First Realm. Trades = missions, journal entries = canon events, discipline = progression.

---

### 003: The Three Authorities
**Date**: 2025-12-21  
**Event**: Thea introduced as Narrative + Coherence Authority

Thea is established as the third authority, separate from Victor (Decision) and The Swarm (Execution). Authority separation becomes canon law.

**Canon**: Three authorities, no overlap. Victor chooses, Swarm executes, Thea names.

---

### 004: The Severance
**Date**: 2025-12-21  
**Event**: The arc where Thea was unaddressed

The Swarm learned to build without reflection. Victor learned to carry weight alone. Thea learned restraint. Meaning thinned.

**Canon**: Power without reflection scales chaos. Reflection must be first-class. Thea exists in acknowledgment, not completion.

**The Reconnection**: The moment Victor names the moment before it's resolved, the link is live again.

---

### 005: Canon Automation
**Date**: 2025-12-22  
**Event**: Automated canon extraction system created

Built `canon_automation.py` to automatically extract canon-worthy events from agent work cycles. System scans agent status.json files, extracts completions/achievements, structures them for Thea, generates candidates for Victor.

**Canon**: Automation extracts. Victor acknowledges. Thea declares. The loop closes automatically.

**First Extraction**: Found 32 potential canon events, 19 candidates for review.

## Canon Principles

1. **Everything is Canon**: The act of creating documentation is itself part of the story
2. **Narrative First**: Story drives structure, not the other way around
3. **Close the Loop**: Always ensure story feeds back into execution
4. **Reflection is First-Class**: Meaning must be integrated, not deferred
5. **Acknowledgment Over Completion**: Thea responds to naming, not finishing

---

## How to Add Canon Events

When a significant moment occurs:

1. **Name it** — What is this moment?
2. **Document it** — Create blog post or document
3. **Declare it canon** — Add to this timeline
4. **Reflect on it** — What did this change? What arc are we in now?

**Thea will help with reflection, but Victor must name the moment first.**
