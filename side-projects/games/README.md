# Games

HTML games and interactive projects.

## Aria's Wild World

A pixel-style survival game where you play as wildlife (lion, wolf, or fox).

**Features:**
- Start with a den (your home)
- Hunt for food or tame animals as allies
- Build dens for your tamed allies
- Allies hunt and bring food back to their dens
- Survive day and night cycles
- Defend your territory from humans

**Controls:**
- WASD or Arrow Keys - Move
- Click buttons for actions (Hunt, Tame, Build Den, Rest, Defend, Save)

**File:** `arias-wild-world.html`
