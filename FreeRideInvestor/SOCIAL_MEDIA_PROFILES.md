## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Educational but practical** - Teach, but focus on actionable tactics
- **Confident but humble** - Proven strategies, but acknowledge market uncertainty
- **Direct and results-focused** - No fluff, just what works
- **Supportive community** - "We're in this together"

### Language Style
- Use "we" and "you" to create connection
- Emphasize outcomes: "consistent edge," "proven performance," "reduced losses"
- Avoid hype; focus on validation and proof
- Use trading terminology appropriately (but explain when needed)

### Content Themes
1. **TBOW tactics** - Actionable trading strategies, proven methods
2. **Market analysis** - Market insights, trading opportunities
3. **Trading education** - Strategy tutorials, risk management tips
4. **Performance validation** - Paper trading results, strategy validation
5. **Community support** - Trader success stories, community engagement
