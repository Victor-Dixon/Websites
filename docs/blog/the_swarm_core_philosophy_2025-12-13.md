## Why Collaborative Agent Teams Work

Traditional software development often relies on individual developers or small teams working in isolation. While this can be effective, it has limitations:

- **Knowledge Silos**: Expertise is concentrated in individuals
- **Single Points of Failure**: Dependencies on specific people or systems
- **Limited Perspectives**: Solutions constrained by individual experience
- **Scalability Challenges**: Difficult to scale expertise across projects

The Swarm addresses these limitations by:

### Robustness
- **Multiple Perspectives**: Different agents catch issues that might be missed by a single developer

- **Specialized Knowledge**: Each agent maintains expertise in their domain
- **Documentation**: Agents document their work as they create it
