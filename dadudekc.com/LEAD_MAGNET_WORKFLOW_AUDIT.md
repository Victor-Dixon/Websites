## Find the 1-3 Workflows Stealing 5-15 Hours/Week

If you're running a service business (or doing everything yourself), the problem usually isn't "lack of tools." It's that **workflows are leaking time**: follow-ups, scheduling, invoicing, handoffs.

This free audit helps you identify exactly which workflows are stealing your time and what to automate first.

## Workflow Audit Checklist

### 1. Follow-Up Workflows

**Questions to Ask:**
- [ ] How many hours/week do you spend on follow-up emails?
- [ ] Do you manually track who needs follow-up?
- [ ] Are you sending the same follow-up emails repeatedly?
- [ ] Do you lose track of who you've contacted?

**Time Leak Indicators:**
- Spending 2+ hours/day on follow-ups
- Manually checking who needs follow-up
- Copy-pasting the same email templates
- Losing track of conversation history

**Automation Opportunity:** Automated follow-up sequences, CRM integration, email templates

### 2. Scheduling Workflows

**Questions to Ask:**
- [ ] How many back-and-forth emails does it take to schedule a meeting?
- [ ] Do you manually check calendar availability?
- [ ] Are you sending availability options via email?
- [ ] Do clients reschedule frequently, requiring manual updates?

**Time Leak Indicators:**
- 5+ emails to schedule one meeting
- Manually checking calendar conflicts
- Sending availability lists via email
- Manual rescheduling coordination

**Automation Opportunity:** Calendar booking links, automated scheduling, time zone handling

### 3. Invoicing Workflows

**Questions to Ask:**
- [ ] How long does it take to create and send an invoice?
- [ ] Do you manually track payment status?
- [ ] Are you sending payment reminders manually?
- [ ] Do you manually reconcile payments?

**Time Leak Indicators:**
- 30+ minutes per invoice creation
- Manual payment tracking spreadsheets
- Sending payment reminders one-by-one
- Manual payment reconciliation

**Automation Opportunity:** Automated invoicing, payment tracking, reminder sequences

### 4. Handoff Workflows

**Questions to Ask:**
- [ ] How many handoffs happen between team members?
- [ ] Do you manually pass information between people?
- [ ] Are handoffs documented or verbal?
- [ ] Do things get lost in handoffs?

**Time Leak Indicators:**
- Information lost in handoffs
- Manual information passing
- Verbal handoffs (not documented)
- Delays waiting for handoffs

**Automation Opportunity:** Automated handoff workflows, task assignment, status updates

### 5. Data Entry Workflows

**Questions to Ask:**
- [ ] How much time do you spend copying data between systems?
- [ ] Do you manually enter the same information multiple times?
- [ ] Are you transferring data from forms to spreadsheets to CRM?
- [ ] Do you manually update multiple systems?

**Time Leak Indicators:**
- Copying data between 3+ systems
- Entering same information multiple times
- Manual data transfer from forms
- Updating multiple systems manually

**Automation Opportunity:** Data sync between systems, form-to-CRM automation, single source of truth
