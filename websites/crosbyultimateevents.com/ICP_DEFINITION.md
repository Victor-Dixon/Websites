## Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary ICP: Affluent Professionals

**Demographics:**
- **Income:** $150K+ annually
- **Age:** 35-65 years old
- **Location:** Urban/suburban areas (Houston metro and surrounding areas)
- **Occupation:** Executives, professionals, business owners, high-net-worth individuals

**Psychographics:**
- Value time and quality over cost
- Want impressive experiences for guests
- Prefer stress-free solutions
- Appreciate attention to detail
- Value professional service

**Characteristics:**
- Busy schedules, limited time for event planning
- High standards for quality and presentation
- Willing to invest in premium experiences
- Social/professional networks where events matter
- Value convenience and peace of mind

### Secondary ICP: Corporate Clients

**Demographics:**
- **Company Size:** Mid to large corporations
- **Department:** Executive teams, HR, marketing, client relations
- **Budget:** Corporate event budgets ($5K-$50K+)

**Psychographics:**
- Need impressive client entertainment
- Value professional representation
- Require reliable, consistent service
- Want seamless execution
- Focus on ROI and business outcomes

**Characteristics:**
- Regular event needs (quarterly, annual)
- Multiple decision-makers involved
- Need detailed proposals and contracts
- Value vendor relationships
- Require professional documentation

## Pain Points

### Primary Pain Points:
1. **Event Planning Stress:** Overwhelmed by coordination tasks
2. **Time Constraints:** Too busy to manage all event details
3. **Vendor Management:** Difficulty coordinating multiple vendors
4. **Quality Concerns:** Fear things won't go perfectly
5. **Lack of Expertise:** Don't know how to plan premium events

### Secondary Pain Points:
1. **Venue Limitations:** Restricted by venue catering options
2. **Generic Service:** Want unique, personalized experiences
3. **Coordination Headaches:** Managing chef, planner, decorator separately
4. **Last-Minute Issues:** Need reliable backup plans
5. **Cost Uncertainty:** Unclear pricing, surprise costs

## Desired Outcomes

### Primary Outcomes:
1. **Flawless Events:** Everything goes perfectly, guests are impressed
2. **Peace of Mind:** Stress-free, can enjoy the event
3. **Time Back:** Don't have to manage logistics
4. **Memorable Experience:** Guests rave about the event
5. **Professional Representation:** Events reflect their standards

### Secondary Outcomes:
1. **One Trusted Partner:** Single point of contact for everything
2. **Seamless Coordination:** All vendors work together perfectly
3. **Predictable Costs:** Clear pricing, no surprises
4. **Scalable Solution:** Can handle events of any size
5. **Long-term Relationship:** Trusted partner for future events
