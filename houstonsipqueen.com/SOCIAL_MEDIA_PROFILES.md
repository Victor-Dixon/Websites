## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Luxury but approachable** - Premium service, accessible communication
- **Southern hospitality** - Warm, welcoming, gracious
- **Professional and confident** - "We've got this, you can relax"
- **Detail-oriented** - Emphasize craft and attention to detail

### Language Style
- Use "we" and "you" to create connection
- Emphasize outcomes: "guests rave," "stress-free hosting," "professional service"
- Avoid jargon; use clear, benefit-focused language
- Include Southern hospitality touches (for Houston market)

### Content Themes
1. **Craft cocktails** - Signature drinks, cocktail recipes, mixology
2. **Event highlights** - Event showcases, client success stories
3. **Behind-the-scenes** - Bar setup, bartenders at work, preparation
4. **Planning tips** - Event bar planning advice, cocktail selection tips
5. **Southern hospitality** - Service excellence, guest experience
